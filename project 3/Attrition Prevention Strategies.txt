Attrition Prevention Strategies:

1. Compensation & Benefits
   - Benchmark salaries against industry standards
   - Offer performance-based bonuses and equity options
   - Provide healthcare, insurance, and retirement benefits

2. Career Growth & Learning
   - Create clear promotion pathways
   - Provide continuous learning programs (courses, certifications, mentoring)
   - Encourage internal mobility across roles

3. Recognition & Engagement
   - Implement recognition programs (awards, peer kudos)
   - Celebrate milestones (work anniversaries, project completions)
   - Use pulse surveys to measure engagement and act on feedback

4. Work-Life Balance
   - Offer flexible schedules and remote work options
   - Encourage reasonable workloads and limit overtime
   - Provide wellness programs (mental health support, gym memberships)

5. Manager-Employee Relationship
   - Train managers in empathy, coaching, and communication
   - Conduct regular one-on-one check-ins
   - Build a culture of trust and transparency
